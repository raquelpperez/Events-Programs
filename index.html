Nature and Poetry: Environmental Justice Listening Session

Friday, April 7, 2023 6:00 PM 8:00 PM Join us on Friday April 7 at 6pm for an Environmental Justice/Activism Listening Session at The Discovery Center! Hosted by Amani Reid, Policy Specialist for PA Interfaith Power and Light, and Audubon Mid-Atlantic. The theme will be Environmental Justice and Activism. This will be an evening to learn, explore, and focus on ways we can get involved and volunteer locally on environmental issues affecting their community like pollution, housing, utilities and more! We will also provide journals to use in discussion as well as when you are exploring the space. Refreshments will be provided. Native Container Garden Workshop

Saturday, April 8, 2023 10:00 AM 12:00 PM Join Audubon Mid-Atlantic plant expert Jose Santiago as we walk you through the ins and outs of growing native plants. You don't need a yard, or a ton of space to grow beautiful, and beneficial, native plants. All you need is a windowsill, a patio, or even a stoop. This workshop is designed to teach you about caring for plants in your home, discuss the benefits of native plants in your community, and even get a free plant to take home. No previous gardening experience necessary! Great for the whole family. This workshop will include a small hike in our front pollinator garden and the beginning of our nature trail. All equipment needed for the workshop will be provided.

Audubon Bird Tour

Sunday, April 16, 2023 8:00 AM 10:00 AM Join Audubon Mid-Atlantic as we give you a bird tour of the Strawberry Mansion Reservoir. Led by local ornithologist Keith Russell, this tour takes you around the reservoir to find the species residing, or just passing though, The Discovery Center. Over 130 species of bird of been spotted at The Discovery Center, including breathtaking species like Bald Eagles. Join us for a beautiful and exciting hike looking for local birdlife. All ages and abilities welcome. Binoculars available for loan.
